about

Hey there! My name is Emma (she/her).

I am a beginner web developer based in New Jersey. My goal is to build beautiful, simple, and accessible websites for everyone.

Before I built websites, I played the clarinet and other woodwind instruments. While I loved getting my bachelor's degree in music performance, I realize now a career in music isn't plausible for me anymore. My passion for music will never go away, but now I can pursue another passion of mine: coding! I've been hooked ever since I took an Intro to Computer Science class while working on my music degree.

Thanks for coming to my portfolio website! If you want to contact me please email me at emgrey02@gmail.com. Love you!

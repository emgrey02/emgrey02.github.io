About

Emma grew up playing the clarinet through middle school and high school, so she followed her dream to go to music school for clarinet performance. After receiving a Bachelor of Music degree from Ithaca College in 2019, Emma realized she could not make a living by playing the clarinet. She needed another plan. Luckily, Emma learned she was passionate about coding after taking an Intro to Computer Science class during her undergraduate studies. It is there she learned the basics of coding with Python. Since graduating, Emma delved into learning web development through free online courses like Harvard's CS50, freecodecamp, and The Odin Project. Recently Emma began working towards an Associate of Applied Science degree in Interface Design and Web Development.
